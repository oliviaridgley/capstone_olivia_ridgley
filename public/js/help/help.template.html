FOLKWAYS

In 1997 Arthur Aron conducted a Social Psychology study to create 'closeness' under controlled circumstances.
This study was later picked up in a Modern Love column by The New York Times and rebranded as 'The 36 Questions to Fall in Love'.
Folkways is inspired by this idea, that honest conversation can lead to closer interpersonal relationships.

Use Folkways :

Play through the original questions.
Browse user created decks of questions.
Create your own set with art curated by the Rijksmuseum
